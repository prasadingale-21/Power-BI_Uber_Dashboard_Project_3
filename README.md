# Power-BI_Uber_Dashboard_Project_3
Uber Dashboard using Power BI

* Designed and developed a multi-page Uber operations analytics dashboard using Power BI, integrating KPIs such as completed bookings, lost bookings, revenue, customer count, and total distance to provide real-time business insights.

* Built interactive visualizations and drill-through pages (Vehicle, Revenue, Rider, Location) to analyze ride patterns by vehicle type, customer behavior, payment method, cancellation reasons, and geographic hotspots.

* Engineered advanced DAX measures, data modeling, and slicer-based filter panels (vehicle type, time period, location, rider type) to enable dynamic exploration of operational performance across months and quarters.

* Implemented behavioral and operational insights, including busy time slots, high-demand locations, customer ratings, cancellation analysis, and revenue contribution by customer segments—supporting data-driven decision-making for mobility operations.
